Bernese mountain dog

Bernese mountain dog, is a large breed of dog. It is tricolored and has long legs. It is sturdy, intelligent, and agile. It was originally bred to work in mountainous areas, where it excelled at tasks such as pulling carts or herding livestock. The breed originated in Europe and was historically an excellent companion for shepherds. Over time, it has become a beloved breed for companionship. In the early days, it was developed in the Bern region of Switzerland for hauling goods, cheese production, and pulling carts for weaving shops.
